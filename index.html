About Us

Mohan Skin & Gastro Care

Welcome to Mohan Skin & Gastro Care — a dual-specialty clinic committed to delivering expert care in dermatology and gastroenterology. Led by Dr. Anush Mohan, an experienced Gastrointestinal Surgeon, and Dr. Vidya S., a skilled Dermatologist, our clinic combines compassionate care with modern medical expertise — all under one roof. Whether you're seeking relief from digestive concerns or looking to enhance your skin health, we provide personalized, results-focused treatments tailored to your unique needs — ensuring comfort, clarity, and confidence throughout your health journey.

DERMATOLOGY

Our dermatology department offers comprehensive skin care solutions tailored to all age groups. Whether you're dealing with acne, eczema, psoriasis, fungal infections, or chronic skin conditions, our expert dermatologists provide advanced diagnosis and personalized treatments. We also specialize in mole evaluation, skin cancer screening, and hyperpigmentation treatments using cutting-edge technology. Restore your skin’s health with medically approved methods that deliver lasting results.

COSMETOLOGY

Achieve radiant, youthful skin with our customized cosmetology services. Our treatments include anti-aging therapies, chemical peels, medical facials, microneedling, skin brightening, and hydration therapies tailored to your unique skin type. We focus on enhancing your natural features using non-invasive aesthetic procedures backed by clinical research and safe practices.

LASER

Our clinic features the latest FDA-approved laser technology for precise and effective aesthetic procedures. We provide laser hair removal, tattoo removal, laser resurfacing for scars, stretch marks, freckles, and skin tightening treatments. Our certified professionals ensure a painless, safe experience with visible improvements in skin texture, tone, and clarity.

GASTROENTEROLOGY

Our gastroenterology unit offers diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of digestive disorders, including acid reflux, GERD, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), fatty liver, ulcers, gastritis, bloating, indigestion, and constipation. We also offer endoscopy and colonoscopy services for accurate diagnosis. Our experienced gastroenterologists work closely with each patient to design holistic, long-term gut health plans.
